What is this

This is a tool that helps you keep up with your lore. You can ask questions about your campaign and the lore keeper will try to answer them as best as possible. If the information you ask is not in the lore keeper's database, it will tell you clearly so. This way you can improve your lore, or just answer "quickly" to your players.

How does it work

The Lore Keeper is a tool that uses a machine learning model to answer questions about your lore. But how can you teach him your lore?
You can load PDFs files in the "Load Docs" section, and the Lore Keeper will split them, read them and learn from them by summarizing the documents, extracting keywords and storing the information in an internal memory.

(Temporary) Limits

Yep unfortunately the Lore Keeper has some limits that I plan to address in the future :
  • It can only read PDF files
  • Uploading the documents is a computationally expensive operation: the smaller the documents the quicker it will be to upload them!
  • As for now it can only load one document a time
  • When you ask questions, the lore keeper will search among the saved documents, please expect some loading times and some freezing (you are running an AI model on your browser)
  • A GPU is needed to run the model

Works Offline!

The Lore Keeper works without any connection, so even in the deepest of the dungeons you can find answers to your questions. You will have to be online only the first time you load the page, to download the model.
