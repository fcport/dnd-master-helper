What is this

This is a tool that helps you keep up with your lore. You can ask questions about your campaign and the lore keeper will try to answer them as best as possible. If the information you ask is not in the lore keeper's database, it will tell you clearly so. This way you can improve your lore, or just answer "quickly" to your players.
